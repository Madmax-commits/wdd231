Website Project Plan

Site Name

RIT Digital Solutions

This name was chosen to reflect the brand identity of RIT Energy Media & Tech Service Consultant, emphasizing modern digital solutions for faster results.

Optional domain availability: ritdigitalsolutions.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of RIT Digital Solutions is to showcase consulting services in technology, media, and digital transformation. The site will highlight service categories such as Digital Development, Business Systems & Integration, Media & AI, and Training & Consulting. It will provide a professional platform for clients to learn about offerings and book consultations.

Scenarios

  • What digital solutions can help my business integrate modern systems?
  • How can I book a consultation with RIT Energy Media & Tech Service Consultant?
  • What training programs are available for adopting AI-driven tools?

Color Scheme

Main Background: #f9fafc

Headings: #004aad

Accent: #ff6f3c

Typography

  • Montserrat – used for headings (modern, professional look)
  • Roboto – used for body text (clean and readable)

CSS

Supporting CSS files will ensure responsive design, consistent color scheme, and typography. Flexbox and CSS Grid will be used for layout, with media queries for mobile responsiveness.

Testing

  • HTML Validation: W3C Markup Validator
  • Accessibility: WAVE Web Accessibility Evaluation Tool
  • Best Practices, SEO & Performance: Google PageSpeed Insights / Chrome DevTools Lighthouse
  • Color Contrast: WebAIM Contrast Checker
